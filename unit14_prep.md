## Key Concepts

### Letter Writing
A written message sent via mail.

**Types:**
- **Formal:** Structured, professional (business, official).
- **Informal:** Personal, casual.
- **Business:** Commercial correspondence.
- **Official:** Government/institutional info.
- **Social:** Invitations, condolences.
- **Circular:** Mass announcements.
- **Employment:** Job-related.
- **Letter of Appeal:** Complaints/redress.
- **Letter of Apology:** Regret for wrongdoing.
- **Letter of Complaint:** Express dissatisfaction.
- **Professional Email:** Electronic formal.
- **Letter of Resignation:** Job quitting.
- **Cover Letter:** Job application.
- **Letter of Recommendation:** Endorsement.

**Format (Formal):**
- Sender’s address
- Date
- Receiver’s address
- Subject
- Salutation
- Body (Introduction, Main, Conclusion)
- Complimentary close
- Signature

**Tips:**
- Identify type
- Open/close correctly
- Establish intent
- Careful language
- Appropriate length

**Importance:**
- Thoughtful communication
- Penmanship practice
- Tangible keepsakes
- Builds relationships

### Notice Writing
Formal announcement displayed publicly.

**Format:**
- Name of Entity
- NOTICE
- Heading
- Body
- Name
- Signature
- Designation

**Important Points:**
- Clear title
- Organization name
- Brief heading
- Concise body
- Date, details
- Proofread
- Unambiguous

### Conveying Sympathies
Expressing condolence.

**Formal Ways:**
- Please accept my deepest condolences...
- Words seem inadequate...
- May the love of those around you...

**Informal:**
- I'm so sorry for your loss...
- My thoughts are with you...

**Tips:**
- Sincere
- Supportive
- Avoid clichés if possible

## Key Terms
- **Formal Letter:** Professional correspondence
- **Informal Letter:** Personal
- **Notice:** Public announcement
- **Condolence:** Sympathy message

## Self Assessment Questions
1. There are broadly_______ types of letters. (B. 2)
2. Business letters fall under the category of_________ letters. (B. formal)
3. Which of the following is a social letter? (C. Condolence letter)
4. Informal letters are also known as________ letters. (A. Personal)
5. Which of the following is not included in the format of a formal letter? (B. Best wishes)
6. __________ is a customary greeting to the recipient of the letter. (C. Salutation)
7. A_______ is a formal document that aims to communicate or display information to the public or a specific group of people. (A. notice)
8. Which of the following is not included in the format of a notice? (B. Salutation)
9. How should the notice end? (D. All of these)
10. A________ letter is a type of letter written to address any type of wrong doing. (A. complaint)
11. The tone and content of a complaint letter should be_________. (B. formal)
12. Which of the following is a complimentary close in a complaint letter? (C. Yours sincerely)
13. The word condolence comes from the Latin word___________. (A. condolere)
14. Besides sending a condolence message, which of the following things can you do to convey your sympathies? (B. Sending flowers with a sympathy card)
15. A letter of___________ is a letter written by one party to another to thank the former at a personal level. (A. appreciation)
