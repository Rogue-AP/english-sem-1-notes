# Unit 10 Exam Preparation: Reading Skills

## Objectives
After studying this unit, you will be able to:
- Realize the significance of reading skills
- Become well versed with the problems faced in interpreting different kinds of texts such as narrative, descriptive and extrapolative texts

## Key Concepts

### Importance of Reading
- Educational and entertaining
- Improves language skills (listening, speaking, writing)
- Familiarizes with English rhythm
- Builds vocabulary and grammar
- Allows self-paced learning

### Reading Skills
- **Skimming:** Glance for main idea (first/last paragraphs, headings)
- **Scanning:** Search for specific information
- **Intensive:** Detailed reading for comprehension
- **Extensive:** Reading for pleasure

### Components of Reading
- **Phonemic Awareness:** Manipulate sounds
- **Phonics:** Letter-sound relationships
- **Fluency:** Speed, accuracy, expression
- **Vocabulary:** Word knowledge
- **Comprehension:** Understanding text

**Strategies for Comprehension:**
- Use prior knowledge
- Predict
- Identify main idea and summarize
- Question
- Draw inferences
- Visualize

### Different Types of Texts

**Narrative Texts:**
- Tells a story
- Structure: Orientation, Complication, Resolution
- Features: Action verbs, temporal connectives, dialogue

**Descriptive Texts:**
- Describes location, object, person
- Objective (informative) or subjective (emotional)
- Features: Spatial indicators, adjectives, present/past verbs

**Extrapolative Texts:**
- Make inferences from given information
- Strategies: Inferential comprehension, critical analysis

## Key Terms
- **Skimming:** Quick overview
- **Scanning:** Specific search
- **Intensive Reading:** Detailed analysis
- **Extensive Reading:** Pleasure reading
- **Comprehension:** Understanding meaning
