## Key Concepts

### Writing Skills
- Grammar, Spelling, Punctuation: Correct usage for clarity and impression.
- Importance: Clear communication, persuasion, persuasion.

### Different Types of Writing
- **Expository:** Explains facts logically (textbooks, essays).
- **Persuasive:** Influences opinion (advertising, reviews).
- **Descriptive:** Engages senses (fiction, poetry).
- **Narrative:** Tells story (novels, biographies).

### Components of the Writing Process
- **Prewriting:** Brainstorm, research, outline.
- **Writing:** Draft first version.
- **Revising:** Add/remove/rearrange for flow.
- **Editing:** Check grammar, spelling, punctuation.
- **Publishing:** Share final work.

### Cohesion and Coherence
- **Cohesion:** Links elements (grammatical, lexical).
- **Coherence:** Logical, understandable (macro level).

**Cohesive Devices:**
- Collocations, lexical repetition, linking adverbials, substitution, ellipsis, conjunctions, synonymy/antonymy, hypernyms/hyponyms, referencing (anaphoric, cataphoric, deictic).

**Examples:**
- Anaphoric reference: Pronouns referring back.
- Cataphoric reference: Words referring forward.
- Tense agreement: Consistent tenses.
- Linkers: And, but, therefore.
- Substitution: Replace words (e.g., do for repeat).
- Synonyms: Similar words for variety.

### Sentence Expanding
Process of adding words, phrases, clauses to main clause.

**Examples:**
- Add adjective clauses
- Add adverb clauses
- Add absolute clauses

**Tips:**
- Add adjectives, verbs, connectors carefully.
- Avoid overloading sentences.

## Key Terms
- **Cohesion:** Micro-level linking
- **Coherence:** Macro-level understanding
- **Expository Writing:** Informative
- **Persuasive Writing:** Argumentative
- **Sentence Expansion:** Elaborating sentences

## Self Assessment Questions
1. Which of the following is not an important aspect of writing skills? (A. Intonation)
2. There are mainly______ types of writing. (B. 4)
3. Expository writing cannot be found in_________. (C. poetry)
4. Persuasive writing can be found in_________. (D. advertising)
5. ___________writing can be found in poetry. (C. Descriptive)
6. Cohesion refers to the many ways in which the elements of a text are________ together. (A. linked)
7. Can a text be internally cohesive but incoherent? (B. Yes)
8. Which of the following is not a cohesive device? (C. Accent)
9. Which cohesive device can you use to introduce an item in a series? (D. First)
10. Which cohesive device can you use to draw a comparison in a text? (A. Moreover)
11. Which of the following is not a way to expand a sentence? (A. Add phonetic code)
12. Should you add many adjectives, verbs and connectors to expand a sentence? (B. No)
13. A prepositional phrase often appears________ the word it modifies. (C. after)
14. An___________ is a word or group of words that identifies or renames another word in a sentence. (D. appositive)
15. __________ appositives begin with a word such as not, never or rather than. (A. Negative)
